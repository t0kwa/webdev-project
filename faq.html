Account
  • How do I change my password? To change your password, go to your account and click change password. Fill in your old password and then create a new password.
  • How do I update my account information? To update your account information, go to your account and fill in all the necessary information.
  • Can I create multiple child accounts? You must have a premium account to create up to 5 child accounts. To create a new child account, go to your account then press add a new child account.
